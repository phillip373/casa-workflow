The three journeys

Each chapter of the animation, step by step
1

Website form

Guest / Agent inquires
  1. Guest or agent submits the website booking form.
  2. A lead is created in Odoo PMS.
  3. A quotation is prepared automatically from the pricelist.
  4. The quotation is sent; the guest reviews and confirms.
  5. The sales order is confirmed.
→ Folio & guest tasks opened · booking confirmed
2

Chatbot

Guest / Agent inquires
  1. Guest or agent chats with the AI assistant.
  2. The chatbot collects booking details (dates, rooms, dives).
  3. It pulls live prices from the Odoo pricelist.
  4. If the guest is ready to book, the details flow into Odoo as a quotation; if not, the conversation ends as a lead to nurture.
  5. Guest confirms → sales order confirmed.
→ Folio & guest tasks opened · booking confirmed
3

OTA direct

Guest / Agent books
  1. Guest books directly on Booking.com / Trip.com.
  2. The OTA confirms the booking and takes payment.
  3. The Channel Manager maps the room & rate plan.
  4. Availability is synced back to every OTA — the night closes everywhere.
  5. A confirmed sales order is created in Odoo automatically.
→ Folio & guest tasks opened · booking confirmed

Channel Manager 101

Chapter 4 of the animation — the middle swimlane, explained
What is a Channel Manager?
  • One calendar connecting Odoo PMS to every OTA.
  • Pushes rates & availability to Booking.com / Trip.com.
  • Pulls confirmed OTA bookings back automatically.
  • Stops double bookings between OTAs & direct sales.
Does Casa need one? Who & cost
  • Worth it once rooms sell on 2+ channels at the same time.
  • Odoo-friendly picks: WuBook, Beds24, SiteMinder, Cloudbeds.
  • Indicative cost: ~USD 20–150 / month (rooms × channels).
  • Without one: staff updates each OTA extranet by hand.
What does “Sync availability to OTAs” mean?
  • A night sold anywhere closes everywhere within minutes.
  • Rates, stops & restrictions stay aligned on all OTAs.
  • Small inventory = highest overbooking risk → yes, needed.
  • Manual updates only work with very few bookings.
Bottom line for Casa
  • The Booking.com share of total bookings is the key number.
  • High share + manual re-typing today = it pays for itself.
  • Low share + few bookings = manual updates are fine for now.
