The three journeys

Each chapter of the animation, step by step
1

Website form

Guest / Agent inquires
  1. Guest or agent submits the website booking form.
  2. A lead is created in Odoo PMS.
  3. A quotation is prepared automatically from the pricelist.
  4. The quotation is sent; the guest reviews and confirms.
  5. The sales order is confirmed.
→ Folio & guest tasks opened · booking confirmed
2

Chatbot

Guest / Agent inquires
  1. Guest or agent chats with the AI assistant.
  2. The chatbot collects booking details (dates, rooms, dives).
  3. It pulls live prices from the Odoo pricelist.
  4. If the guest is ready to book, the details flow into Odoo as a quotation; if not, the conversation ends as a lead to nurture.
  5. Guest confirms → sales order confirmed.
→ Folio & guest tasks opened · booking confirmed
3

OTA direct

Guest / Agent books
  1. Guest books directly on Booking.com / Trip.com.
  2. The OTA confirms the booking and takes payment.
  3. The Channel Manager maps the room & rate plan.
  4. Availability is synced back to every OTA — the night closes everywhere.
  5. A confirmed sales order is created in Odoo automatically.
→ Folio & guest tasks opened · booking confirmed

What is a Channel Manager?

The middle swimlane, explained

One calendar between Odoo and every OTA

The Channel Manager sits between Odoo PMS and the OTAs: rates and availability flow out to every channel, confirmed OTA bookings flow back in as sales orders — so the calendar is always one single source of truth.
